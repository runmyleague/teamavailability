Team Availability Calendar

for Red Test 11

Step-by-Step: Team Availability Set-Up
Teams must complete ALL steps. Upon completion of any step, it will change colors (red to green). Some steps are dependent on previous steps and will not become available until those steps are completed.
  1. Enter/Modify Global Inputs  Click Here to Edit   Max Games = 50
  2. Set Days with Travel Restrictions  Click Here to Edit
  3. Construct/Modify Typical Weekly Pattern (default pattern assigned to all weeks as starting point)
  4. Change Pattern For Any Given Week  Click Here to Build a New Pattern or Edit Existing Pattern OR Change the pattern assigned to any week by clicking on the edit icon () on the calendar below.
  5. E-Verify Availability Calendar - Congratulations, You're Done!

When filling out your team availability calendar, be sure to block out any dates or times where your team will be unable to play because of conflicts with other events such as:

  1. Holidays ---> Memorial Day Weekend or the 4th of July
  2. School Exams ---> Night before SAT or Finals
  3. Other School Events ---> Sports Banquets, Graduation, Dances, Proms, Class Trips.
  4. Non-availability of Manager/Coaches ---> Weddings, Baptisms, Planned Trips.
